About This Tool

This system uses a combination of deep learning models to analyze brain MRI scans:

  • VGG16 Model: Classifies images as tumor or non-tumor
  • YOLO Model: Detects tumor regions with bounding boxes
  • SAM (Segment Anything Model): Creates precise segmentation masks

Note: This tool is for research and educational purposes. Always consult medical professionals for clinical diagnoses.
